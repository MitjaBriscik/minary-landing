Why we built Minary.

Working alone makes every day a prioritization problem.

Freelancers, researchers, founders, creators, and students know the feeling: too many open loops, no manager, no team rhythm, and no clear answer to what deserves your best energy today.

The problem was never the list.
Task managers remember your tasks, but they do not understand the headspace you're in.
Tasks Time Energy Deadlines Control Focus What to do now
Our answer

Minary plans around you, not just your tasks.

Today

Minary turns your tasks, time, energy, and sense of control into a focused work session: what to do now, what to postpone, and how to make progress without spending the morning deciding.

Where it's going

We are building Minary into a daily AI workspace: morning check-ins, tasks from chat, calendar-aware planning, long-term project memory, and an assistant that learns how you actually work.
