Why we are building Minary

Working alone makes every day a prioritization problem.

Freelancers and founders, makers and creators, artists and developers, researchers and students know it: too many open tasks, but no manager nor structure to keep things on track. This translates into two problems: first how to organize the list of tasks into a plan, and second what to do with today's time.

Task managers remember your tasks, but they do not understand the headspace you're in.
Our answer

Minary organizes your tasks,
then matches the right one for every session.

Structure your To-Do list into a plan 1

Describe your work and your goals, and Minary turns them into a structured project: what to do, in what order, by when.

Let Minary choose your next task 2

Tell it your time and energy, and Minary picks the right task for the session, so you don't waste time deciding.
