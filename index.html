The problem

Independent workers know that turning a messy project in your head into an ordered plan isn't easy. And working alone makes every day a prioritization problem: too many open tasks, no manager and no structure to keep things on track.

Our answer

Task managers remember your tasks, but they don't understand the headspace you're in.

1 Structure your goals into a clear plan

Describe what's on your plate, and Minary crafts a structured project: what to do, in what order, by when.

3 The learning layer

With every session, Minary learns how you work: your strengths, weaknesses and habits. So its plans become increasingly tailored to you.

Learning loop

Every day: Minary plans, you work, then recap how the session went. What Minary learns from your work and your recap sharpens the next plan.
