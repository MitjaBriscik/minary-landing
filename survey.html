Most tools hand you a list and leave the hard part to you: deciding what actually deserves your attention today. Minary works the other way around.

Before each work session, you tell it three things:

  • How much time you have
  • What your energy feels like right now
  • How in control you feel

Minary scores every task against your deadlines and priorities, then hands you one clear plan: what to do now, what to postpone. It gets sharper every session, fitting the day you're actually having.
