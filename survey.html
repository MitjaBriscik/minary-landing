What is Minary

Minary, unlike most tools, follows you from the outline creation of your projects and tasks to the daily planning of your work sessions.

01Setting up the work

If you're an independent worker, you know that turning a messy project in your head into an ordered plan isn't easy. Just describe what you're working on, and Minary turns it into a structured project: what to do, in what order, by when.

02Every day

Before each work session you tell Minary three things:

  • how much time you have
  • what your energy feels like right now
  • and how in control you feel

Minary will read your headspace and your deadlines, and then craft your work session for the day.

03The learning layer

By working daily with Minary, session after session, it learns your strengths and weaknesses, and its plans fit your way of working better and better.
